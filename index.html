Automatically Generated

All bindings to the Material-UI components are automatically generated from the original documentation. They are extracted & rendered by a combination of custom-built typescript tools.

Actively maintained

We (cca.io) use these bindings daily in various production projects. This incentivizes us to regularly update the bindings and to keep breaking changes to a minimum whereever possible.
